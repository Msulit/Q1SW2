Noli Me Tangere Characters

Kapitan Tiago He is very wealthy and owns a big house. He is also friends with many of the church.
Padre Damaso Is the father of Maria Clara. He is also a priest in San Diego.
Don Rafael Ibarra Is the father of Juan Crisostomo Ibarra. He was also a wealthy man in San Diego.
Tenyente Guevarra Is a friend of Don Rafael Ibarra. He was close to Juans father and told him stories.
Juan Crisostomo Ibarra Is a smart, young and wealthy person. He studied at Europe for 7 years.
